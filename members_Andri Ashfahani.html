Andri Ashfahani

Lab Position: Ph.D candidate (since 2019)

Email: andriash001@e.ntu.edu.sg

Office: CI Lab, N4-B1a-02, SCSE, NTU

Research Topic: Autonomous Deep Learning, Continual Learning and Weakly-Supervised Learning

Brief Biography:

Andri Ashfahani is a Ph.D. student in School of Computer Science and Engineering, Nanyang Technological University, Singapore. His research interest covers autonomous deep learning and data stream analytics for the continual learning environment. Previously, he had been a lecturer from 2014 to 2017 in Institut Teknologi Sepuluh Nopember.

Education:
2008 - 2012: BEng, Electrical Engineering, Institut Teknologi Sepuluh Nopember, Indonesia
2013 - 2014: MS, Electrical Engineering, National Taiwan University of Science and Technology, Taiwan
2018 - now: PhD, School of Computer Science and Engineering, Nanyang Technological University, Singapore
