Lab Position: Ph.D. Students (2018, Spring)

Email: fshen@elektro.dtu.dk; feifan001@e.ntu.edu.sg

Office: DTU, Center for Electric Power and Energy

Research Topic: Congestion management and self-healing for distribution networks.

Brief Biography:

Feifan SHEN received the B.Eng. degree in electrical engineering and automation from Hohai University, Nanjing, China, in 2015, and the M.Eng. degree from the Department of Electrical Engineering, Wuhan University, Wuhan, China, in 2017. He is currently working toward the joint Ph.D. degree in the Department of Electrical Engineering, Technical University of Denmark, Denmark, and School of Electrical and Electronic Engineering, Nanyang Technological University, Singapore.
