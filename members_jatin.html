Brief Biography:

Jatin is pursuing research on expediting the integration of renewable energy sources in power systems by developing algorithms to accurately forecast the renewable power generation, like the Wind Turbine and PV generation, with minimum time consumption to be applicable in real time operation. I am also working on a project under ERI@N based on the designing of a hybrid AC-DC Microgrid' at Jurong Port, Singapore
