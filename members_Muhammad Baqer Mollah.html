Brief Biography:

Muhammad Baqer Mollah has been a research associate at School of Computer Science and Engineering in Nanyang Technological University (NTU). Here, he has been affiliated with the Computer Networks and Communications Lab (CNCL) and working under Prof. Dusit Niyato and Prof. Jun Zhao. In his research, he was involved in developing game theory based mechanisms for the intelligent cyber-physical systems (e.g., energy, industry, transportation). His research interests are mainly focused on advanced communication, security, resource allocation, and network economics techniques for future wireless networks and cyber-physical systems. He is passionate about learning, teaching, and research. Besides, he is also serving as a reviewer for several major IEEE journals, magazines, and conferences. He received his MS in Computer Science and BS in Electrical and Electronic Engineering from Jahangirnagar University and International Islamic University Chittagong, Bangladesh, respectively. I am a member of IEEE, IEEE ComSoc, and IEEE VTS.
