Huimei Han

Lab Position: Research Fellow （2021 ~）

Email: hmhan1215@zjut.edu.cn

Brief Biography:

Huimei Han received the Ph.D. degree in telecommunication engineering from Xidian University, Xi’an, China, in 2019. From 2017 to 2018, she was with Florida Atlantic University, USA, as an Exchange Ph.D. Student. Since 2019, she has been with the College of Information Engineering, Zhejiang University of Technology and the School of Computer Science and Engineering, Nanyang Technological University, Singapore, as a Research Fellow, since 2021. Her current research interests include random access schemes for massive MIMO systems, machine-to-machine communications, machine learning, intelligent reflecting surface, and federated learning. er Ph.D. degree in telecommunication engineering from Xidian University, Xi'an, P.
