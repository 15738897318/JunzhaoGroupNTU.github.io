Brief Biography:

Song Qun received B.S. (2018) in Computer Science from Nankai University. Currently, she is a Ph.D. student co-supervised by Asst Prof. Rui Tan (SCSE) and Asst Prof. Yan Xu (EEE) under the Energy Research Institute @ NTU (ERI@N).
