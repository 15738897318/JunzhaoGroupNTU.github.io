Email: HONGYUAN001@E.NTU.EDU.SG

Research Topic: Data Driven Home Energy Management System

Brief Biography:

Hongyuan Ding received his B.S. degree in electrical engineering and automation from Zhejiang University, China in 2018, and the M.Sc. degrees in computer control and automation from the School of Electrical and Electronic Engineering, Nanyang Technological University, Singapore, in 2019. Currently he is with Nanyang Technological University, Singapore, as a research associate, pursuing Ph.D. degree. His research interests include home energy management system, residential level load forecasting, non-intrusive load monitoring.
