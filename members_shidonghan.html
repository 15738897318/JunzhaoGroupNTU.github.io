Brief Biography:

Donghan Shi received his B.Eng. degree in electrical engineering from Wuhan University, Wuhan, China, in 2013 and M.Sc. from the School of Electrical and Electronic Engineering, Nanyang Technological University, Singapore in 2014. Since 2014, he joint Nanyang Technological University as a research associate. And now, he is with Energy Research Institute @ Nanyang Technological University (ERI@N), where he works on power electronics and micro-grid control and real-time simulation of power systems.
