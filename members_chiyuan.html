Research Topic: Power System Planning and Resilience

Brief Biography:

Received the B.S. degree M.S degree in electrical engineering from Southeast University and Chongqing University, in 2009 and 2012, respectively. Since 2012, he worked as an electrical engineer of power system planning with State Grid Chongqing Electric Power Research Institute of Chongqing Electric Power Company. Currently he is working toward the Ph.D. degree at Nanyang Technological University. His research interests include power system planning and resilience.
