Email: Sumedha.Sharma@ces.iitd.ac.in

Research Topic: Energy Management in Smart Microgrids, Building Energy Management, Demand Response

Brief Biography:

Sumedha received my B.Tech. degree in Electrical & Electronics Engineering from U.P. Technical University, Lucknow, India in 2013. I received my M.E. degree in Power Systems from Thapar University, Patiala, India in 2016. Currently I am pursuing my Ph.D. at the Centre of Energy Studies, Indian Institute of Technology Delhi, New Delhi, India. As part of my research, I am visiting Nanyang Technological University to work under Prof. Xu Yan for a period of six months. My research interests include renewable energy integration in smart microgrids, demand side management, energy-efficient buildings.
