Xu Qianwen

Lab Position: Research Fellow (2018)

Email: qxu007@e.ntu.edu.sg

Research Topic: Microgrid Energy Management

Brief Biography:

Qianwen Xu received the B.Sc. degree in electrical engineering from Tianjin University, China, in 2014. She is currently working toward the Ph.D. degree in the School of Electrical and Electronic Engineering, Nanyang Technological University, Singapore. Her research interests include microgrid stability and control. She has published 9 technical papers in top SCI-indexed Journals and more than 10 conference papers.

Education:
2010 - 2014: BSc, Qiushi Honors College, Tianjin University, China
2014 - 2018: PhD, School of Electrical and Electronic Engineering, Nanyang Technological University, Singapore
2018 - NOW: Research Fellow, School of Electrical and Electronic Engineering, Nanyang Technological University, Singapore
