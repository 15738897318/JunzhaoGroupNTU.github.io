Chen Zaiyu

Lab Position: Research Fellow (2020, Spring)

Email: zaiyu.chen@ntu.edu.sg

Office: S2-B7c-05, Clean Energy Research Lab

Research Topic: Active Power Control of Wind Turbines

Brief Biography:

Research fellow at School of Electrical & Electronic Engineering, Nanyang Technological University. He received his Ph.D. degree in Control Science and Engineering from Nanjing University of Science & Technology in 2019. His research interest covers maximum power point tracking control of wind turbines, active power control of wind turbines and control strategy for wind turbines participation in system frequency regulation.

Education:
2010 - 2014: BSc, School of Science, Nanjing University of Science and Technology, China
2014 - 2019: PhD, School of Automation, Nanjing University of Science and Technology, China
